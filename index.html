RECOGNITION PROJECT

CELEBRATING TALENT 2025

Executive Summary

A. CURRENT SITUATION

While formal recognition tools like performance bonuses are in place, our day-to-day approach to celebrating achievements remains unstructured. Recognition is often ad-hoc and manager-dependent, lacking an accessible program for immediate feedback. This means we miss the opportunity to consistently and equitably reward the actions that exemplify our company's best practices.

B. CHALLENGES

A more inclusive program could help us address some challenges:

  • Missed opportunities to reinforce positive behaviors: Access to an immediate recognition system can further enhance employee motivation by reinforcing these actions in real-time.
  • Difficulty reinforcing the company's principles in daily operations.
  • Scarcity of mechanisms to celebrate individual and collective successes.

C. OPPORTUNITIES

The "Recognition Project: Celebrating Talent" is a strategic opportunity to:

  • Enhance employee motivation, belonging, and job satisfaction.
  • Promote and give visibility to Clever's best practices: Clever First, Be Bold, Flow with Heart, and Level Up!
  • Strengthen a culture of positive feedback.
  • Act as another valuable tool for talent attraction and retention.
